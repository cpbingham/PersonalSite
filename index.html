About Me

Hello! I'm Colin, a software developer currently based in Toronto, CA who is interested in building things for the modern internet. I'm passionate about working on products that are capable of making a positive, real world impact. My goal is to help promote and contribute to the use and development of software that will help keep the internet safe, free and open to everyone.

I graduated from Queen's University in 2018, where I obtained a degree in Computer Science, specializing in Software Design. After spending the summer travelling, I joined Tata Consultancy Services in September, where I have been working as a software developer since.

In my spare time I most enjoy exercise, being in the outdoors, cooking, playing/listening to music and learning new things.

QAlog

For our degrees capstone project, my team of 3 and I were contracted by Kingston General Hospital to develop a mobile application to allow anesthesiology residents log and evaluate the medical interventions they perform. QAlog was developed for iOS and Android in C# using Xamarin. The primary goal of this application is to provide residents with an effective and user-friendly way to record the anesthetic and echocardiographic operations they've completed as part of their residency.

C# Xamarin Azure

houseMatch

Worked in a 6-person agile team as a part of a Software Development course taught in C++. We designed and programmed an online housing service which connects students looking for temporary housing with students who have available rooms. The application used NodeJS and the REST API to connect clients to a web server which used C++ objects wrapped using Google's v8 Javascript engine library.
